(Phnom Penh): Building on the success of our first CyberAI Explorer initiative, the Committee for Community Service Program (CCSP) proudly launched CyberAI Explorer Cohort 2 at ACT Academy of Careers and Technology. Over two days, our volunteers continued their mission of empowering students with practical knowledge in cybersecurity and artificial intelligence.

Throughout the program, students learned essential cybersecurity concepts, including password security, online privacy, phishing awareness, and safe digital practices. They also explored the growing world of Artificial Intelligence, discovering how to write effective prompts, evaluate AI-generated content, and use AI technologies responsibly and ethically.

The workshops featured interactive activities, real-life examples, and collaborative discussions that encouraged students to think critically about technology and its impact on society. Participants actively engaged in hands-on exercises, strengthening both their technical understanding and problem-solving skills.

Through CyberAI Explorer Cohort 2, CCSP continues its commitment to promoting digital literacy, cybersecurity awareness, and responsible AI adoption, empowering young people with the skills and confidence to become the next generation of innovative and responsible digital citizens.
